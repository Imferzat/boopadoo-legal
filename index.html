Boopadoo Uploader

Our own tool for publishing Boopadoo's pet videos.

What it does

Boopadoo Uploader is a private desktop application we use to publish our own short-form pet videos to our own social media profiles — including our own TikTok account — with consistent captions and branding.

How it works

We sign in with our own account, prepare a vertical (9:16) video, and the tool uploads it directly to our profile through the official platform APIs. It is used only by us, for our own accounts, and does not act on behalf of any other users.
