Privacy Policy — Boopadoo

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Boopadoo (“we”, “us”) operates the “Boopadoo Uploader” application, which we use to publish our own pet-related short videos to our own social media profiles, including our own TikTok account. This policy explains what data the application handles and how.

1. Who this applies to

The Boopadoo Uploader is a private, single-operator tool. It publishes content only to social media accounts that we own and control. It is not offered to, and is not used on behalf of, other end users.

2. Information we access

When we connect our own TikTok account through TikTok Login, the application receives an access token and basic account information (such as our TikTok account identifier) solely to publish videos to that same account. We do not collect, process, or store personal data belonging to any third party.

3. How we use it

The access token is used only to upload and publish video content to the connected account through the TikTok Content Posting API. It is not used for any other purpose, and it is never shared, sold, or transferred to third parties.

4. Storage and security

Access tokens are stored locally on the operator’s own computer in a protected configuration file and are never transmitted to any server other than the official TikTok API endpoints. Tokens can be revoked at any time from the connected account’s security settings.

5. Data retention

We retain the access token only for as long as it is needed to operate the application. Revoking access in the account settings immediately ends our ability to use it.

6. Third-party services

The application interacts only with the official APIs of the platforms we publish to (including TikTok, Meta, and YouTube). Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Children’s privacy

The application is an internal publishing tool and is not directed to children. It does not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us at daboopadoop@gmail.com.
